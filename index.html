About

We're setting up a new community group for the Percival Street Estate to help give a voice to residents and make improvements on the estate.

We're doing this because we will be able to gain access to funding pots and other opportunities that can make our estate a better place to live, for everyone.

The Percival Street Estate includes 253 properties:

  1. Grimthorpe House - 128 properties
  2. Tompion House – 80 properties
  3. Crayle House – 22 properties
  4. Partridge Court – 13 properties
  5. Earnshaw House – 10 properties

If you live on the estate and you're interested in learning more and would like to become a member of the community group, please sign up below and we'll be in touch soon.
